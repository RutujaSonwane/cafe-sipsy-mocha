Our Story

Brewing memories, one cup at a time.

Born from a Late-Night Craving

Cafe Sipsy Mocha started with a simple idea: good coffee shouldn't have a curfew. Founded in 2022 by a group of friends who were tired of having nowhere to go after midnight, we set out to create a space that feels like a second home.

Located in the heart of Kharadi, we've become a sanctuary for night owls, students, and anyone looking for a warm vibe and a hot meal. We believe that the best conversations happen over a cup of coffee at 2 AM.
